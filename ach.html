ATHELETICS

Arya Sinkar from RC Sports Academy won Silver medal in 400m individual running event in West zone Athletics National championship held at Nagpur. She also won a Gold medal in 4X400m Relay for Maharshtra team. This success has opened the door for Arya in National Athletics event to be held at Andhra Pradesh in October. Arya practices at RC Sports academy with the aim of playing for India. She gets the coaching from Sanjay Patankar from Pune.

ARCHERY

Isha pawar (from RC sports academy) wins gold in Khelo india U-17 girls compound archery. 2 students have won Silver Medal in State Level Field Archery (Compound) event held at Nasik in October 2014 Vishnupriya Samant and Sarvashree Sagvekar won the silver medal in the team event.

SHOOTING

2 students have been selected for National Youth Team Qualifiers were held in New Delhi in September 2014.
